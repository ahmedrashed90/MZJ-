MZJ Publish

Web-based social media publishing platform

Connect social accounts and publish approved marketing content.

MZJ Publish is a web-based publishing platform for authorized users to connect social media accounts, manage approved publishing jobs, and publish marketing content to the accounts they are authorized to manage.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service Data Deletion

Account Connection

Authorized users can connect supported social accounts using platform-approved login and authorization flows.

Publishing Workflow

Users create or receive approved publishing jobs containing media, captions, hashtags, platforms, and publishing time.

Publishing Status

The platform records publishing status, errors, and publishing history so authorized users can monitor content delivery.

TikTok Integration

MZJ Publish uses TikTok Login and the TikTok Content Posting API to allow authorized users to connect TikTok accounts they manage and publish approved marketing content through a controlled web-based workflow.

  • TikTok account authorization is performed by the user through TikTok Login.
  • Only accounts authorized by the user are available for publishing.
  • Publishing actions are initiated for approved content jobs managed inside MZJ Publish.
  • The platform does not sell TikTok user data and does not expose access tokens to public frontend code.
