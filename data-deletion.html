Data Deletion Instructions

Application: MZJ Publish

Contact: me.ahmed.rashed@icloud.com

Users can request deletion of their data connected to MZJ Publish by following the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to me.ahmed.rashed@icloud.com with the subject line: MZJ Publish Data Deletion Request.

Please include the connected account email or page/account name so we can identify the relevant records.

What Will Be Deleted

We will delete stored account connection records, access tokens, page/account identifiers, and related publishing history where deletion is legally and technically possible.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are usually processed within 7 business days.

Remove App Access from Facebook

You can also remove the app from your Facebook settings by going to Facebook Settings, then Apps and Websites, and removing MZJ Publish.
