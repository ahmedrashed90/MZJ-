MZJ Publish is a web-based publishing platform that allows authorized users to connect social media accounts and publish approved marketing content to accounts they are authorized to manage.

Information We Collect

When a user connects a social media account, MZJ Publish may receive basic profile information, account identifiers, page or profile identifiers, permissions granted by the user, access tokens required to operate the service, and publishing status data.

TikTok Data Usage

When a user connects a TikTok account, MZJ Publish uses TikTok Login and the TikTok Content Posting API to authenticate the user, identify the connected TikTok account, and publish approved content requested through the platform workflow.

  • We use TikTok data only to authenticate users, show connected account status, and perform authorized publishing actions.
  • We do not sell TikTok user data.
  • We do not use TikTok data for advertising targeting or unrelated analytics.
  • Access tokens are stored securely and are not intended to be exposed in public frontend code.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide account connection, publishing, publishing history, error tracking, and status monitoring features to authorized users.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share user data with third parties except where necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, or interact with the social media platforms authorized by the user.

Data Retention

We retain account connection and publishing data only as long as needed to provide the service, maintain audit records, resolve publishing issues, or comply with legal requirements.

User Rights and Data Deletion

Users may request data deletion by contacting us at me.ahmed.rashed@icloud.com or by visiting our Data Deletion page.
